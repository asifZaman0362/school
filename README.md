# School Projects Repository

Welcome to my School Projects Repository! 📚🖥️ This repository is a curated collection of various projects and assignments I completed during my college journey, as I embarked on the exciting adventure of learning about computers and related technologies. While I may not be a master of tidiness, I've tried my best to organize these projects in the most logical way possible.

## Repository Structure

In the earlier stages, this repository was a bit of a horror story, with everything crammed into one giant mess. However, on one fateful evening, I decided it was time for a change. I broke down the chaos into smaller, more manageable chunks, each with its own repository. These smaller repositories are now included here as submodules, making it much easier to navigate and maintain.

## Why Submodules?

Submodules are a fantastic way to keep things organized while still having individual repositories for each project. This approach allows for better separation, version control, and easier collaboration, while keeping all of my school-related work in one place.

## What You'll Find Here

Inside this repository, you'll discover a diverse range of submodules, each representing a specific project or assignment. These submodules are organized and categorized to the best of my ability, given my ongoing journey towards tidiness. Here's a glimpse of what you can explore:

- **Project A**: A detailed explanation of what this project is about, what technologies were used, and any notable achievements or challenges.

- **Assignment B**: An in-depth look at a challenging assignment I tackled, including the problem statement, my approach, and the final outcome.

- **Research Paper C**: A repository dedicated to a research paper I worked on during my academic journey. Dive into the world of academic exploration.

## How to Navigate

To explore a specific project or assignment, simply click on the corresponding submodule link in this repository. It will take you to the dedicated repository for that item, where you can find detailed code, documentation, and any additional resources.
